THINGS THAT GO BUMP

A variant on the classic Hangman game.


This is Shiver.


Shiver is afraid of EVERYTHING—and for good reason. A rather spicy poker game in the 1900’s landed Shiver‘s family with a curse. Every hundred years, family members are hunted down by Fear, itself, and die from a heart attack—and Shiver is living at the tail end of the past century.

But there’s a catch: If Shiver can anticipate Fear before it materializes, it’s forced to disperse momentarily. Disperse it enough times and it can no longer patch itself together again.


Do you think you can help Shiver beat the curse?
